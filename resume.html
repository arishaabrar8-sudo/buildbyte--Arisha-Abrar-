📄 Resume Builder

Learn how to create a professional ATS-friendly resume that increases your chances of getting internships and job interviews.

Essential Resume Sections

👤 Personal Information

Include your name, email, phone number, LinkedIn profile, GitHub, and portfolio website.

🎓 Education

Mention your degree, university, GPA, graduation year, and relevant coursework.

💻 Skills

Add programming languages, frameworks, databases, tools, and technical skills.

🚀 Projects

Showcase your best academic and personal projects with GitHub links.

🏆 Certifications

Include relevant certifications from Google, Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, IBM, and others.

🤝 Experience

Mention internships, volunteer work, leadership roles, and extracurricular activities.

ATS Resume Tips

  • Keep your resume to one page.
  • Use simple fonts like Arial or Calibri.
  • Avoid tables, images, and graphics.
  • Use keywords from the job description.
  • Highlight measurable achievements.
  • Save your resume as a PDF unless instructed otherwise.

Common Resume Mistakes

  • Spelling and grammar errors.
  • Using an unprofessional email address.
  • Adding unnecessary personal information.
  • Listing every skill without proof.
  • Writing long paragraphs instead of bullet points.
  • Using outdated resume formats.

Resume Checklist

  • ✔ Contact information is correct.
  • ✔ Skills match the job role.
  • ✔ Projects include GitHub links.
  • ✔ No spelling or grammar mistakes.
  • ✔ ATS-friendly format.
  • ✔ Resume saved as PDF.

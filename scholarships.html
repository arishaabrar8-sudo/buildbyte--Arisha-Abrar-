Application Tips

  • Maintain a strong academic record.
  • Prepare a professional CV/Resume.
  • Write an effective Statement of Purpose (SOP).
  • Collect recommendation letters in advance.
  • Apply before the deadline.
  • Read all eligibility requirements carefully.

Documents You'll Need

  • Academic Transcripts
  • CNIC / Passport
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Recommendation Letters
  • English Proficiency Certificate (if required)

🎤 Interview Preparation

Prepare confidently for HR, technical, and coding interviews with common questions, tips, and best practices.

Interview Types

👨‍💼 HR Interview

Learn how to answer behavioral and personality-based interview questions confidently.

💻 Technical Interview

Prepare for programming, databases, software engineering, and system design questions.

🧩 Coding Interview

Practice coding problems on arrays, strings, linked lists, trees, graphs, and algorithms.

Common HR Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why do you want to join our company?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Describe a challenging situation you faced.
  • Where do you see yourself in five years?
  • Why should we hire you?

Technical Interview Topics

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Software Engineering

Interview Tips

  • Research the company before the interview.
  • Practice answering common questions.
  • Dress professionally.
  • Maintain eye contact and confidence.
  • Ask thoughtful questions at the end.
  • Send a thank-you email after the interview.

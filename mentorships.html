Benefits of Having a Mentor

  • 🎯 Set clear academic and career goals.
  • 💡 Learn from real-world experience.
  • 📈 Improve technical and soft skills.
  • 🤝 Expand your professional network.
  • 💼 Prepare for internships and jobs.
  • 🚀 Gain confidence through personalized guidance.

How to Be a Good Mentee

  • Come prepared with questions.
  • Respect your mentor's time.
  • Act on the advice you receive.
  • Be open to constructive feedback.
  • Stay consistent and communicate regularly.
  • Express gratitude and professionalism.

Qualities of a Great Mentor

  • Strong technical knowledge.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Supportive and approachable.
  • Encourages continuous learning.
  • Provides honest feedback.
  • Inspires confidence and growth.
